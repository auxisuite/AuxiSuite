The Nursing Process

The NCP, complete and guided

Auxi Flow walks you through the 5 stages of the Nursing Care Process in a structured way, using the international standard taxonomy.

1. Assessment

Collect patient data systematically with structured forms for assessment using functional health patterns.

2. NANDA Diagnosis

Access the complete NANDA taxonomy. Select nursing diagnoses with their defining characteristics and related factors.

3. NOC Planning

Define patient goals with measurable NOC outcomes. Set indicators and measurement scales for each expected outcome.

4. NIC Interventions

Select NIC interventions corresponding to each diagnosis, with the specific nursing activities for each intervention.

5. Evaluation

Record progress and evaluate the achievement of NOC outcomes. Document patient evolution and adjust the plan as needed.

Integrated Scales & Scores

Assessment tools integrated directly into the nursing process workflow: pain scales, functional assessment, fall risk and more.

Frequently Asked Questions
Are the apps free?
Yes, all Auxi Suite apps are free to download on Google Play and App Store. Some additional features or premium content may be available as optional in-app subscriptions.
Do they work without an internet connection?
Yes. Auxi Medical, Auxi Flow and Calculadoc work completely offline. Once the app is downloaded, you don't need an internet connection to use any of its main features.
What devices are they available on?
Our apps are currently available for Android devices through Google Play and iPhone through App Store.
Are the calculators and data reliable?
All calculators, scales and medication data in Auxi Suite are based on recognized clinical sources and validated scientific publications. All sources are referenced within each app. However, the apps are reference tools and do not replace professional clinical judgment.
How often is the content updated?
We publish periodic updates to add new calculators, correct information and improve the experience. We recommend enabling automatic updates on Google Play and App Store.
How can I report a bug or suggest a calculator?
You can use the contact form on this page or send us an email directly. You can also send us feedback through your app store. We greatly value feedback from healthcare professionals who use our apps.
What is the difference between Auxi Medical and Auxi Flow?
Auxi Medical is the all-in-one nursing app: calculators, medications, guides and a basic care plan generator. Auxi Flow is an app specialized exclusively in the complete Nursing Care Process with taxonomy referenced to NANDA, NOC and NIC. If you need detailed professional care plans, Auxi Flow is the choice; if you need everything in a single app, choose Auxi Medical.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Auxi Suite apps do not collect patient data. All information you enter in the calculators is processed locally on your device and is not sent to any external server. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

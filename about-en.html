Who we are

Built from
the inside

We are an independent development team based in Mexico, driven by a simple conviction: healthcare professionals deserve better tools.

Our story

Born from a
real need

Auxi Suite didn't come out of a boardroom or an investor pitch. It came out of conversations with nurses and healthcare workers who, shift after shift, were looking for digital tools they could actually trust — apps that worked without internet, calculators with accurate results, references that were always there when they needed them most.

The options that existed weren't good enough. They were slow, disconnected from clinical reality, or simply not designed for the pace of a real shift. So we decided to build what we would have wanted ourselves: a complete, reliable, offline-first suite, made for real work in hospitals, clinics and health centers.

Today, Auxi Suite is made up of three applications — Auxi Medical, Auxi Flow and Calculadoc — each one designed for a specific need, and all sharing the same philosophy: clinical precision without compromise, always in your pocket.

Our mission

"To put high-quality clinical tools in the hands of every healthcare professional, no matter where they work or how strong their internet signal is."

What guides us

The principles behind
every decision

Offline first

There isn't always a signal, but there are always patients to care for. All our apps work 100% without an internet connection, because clinical work doesn't wait.

Precision that matters

Every calculator, scale and medication entry is based on recognized scientific sources and reviewed periodically. Because in clinical practice, imprecision is not an option.

Speed without friction

Every screen, every workflow and every interaction is designed for the real pace of a shift. No unnecessary steps. No distractions. The result when you need it.

Always evolving

Medical evidence advances and our apps advance with it. We publish periodic updates to keep content current and add new tools based on user feedback.

Built for real professionals

We don't design for demos or presentations. We design for the nurse on the night shift, the doctor in the ER, and the student on their first clinical rotation.

Mexican roots

We were born in Mexico with Latin America's healthcare system in mind. We know the protocols, the terminology and the context of those who work in our region.

Have feedback
or suggestions?

We care about what healthcare professionals think. Your clinical experience is what guides us to keep improving.

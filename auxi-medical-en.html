Features

Everything you need,
always available

Auxi Medical brings together the most important tools for daily nursing work in a single clear, fast and reliable app.

Clinical Calculators & Scales

More than 20 essential clinical calculators and scales for nursing, with instant results and clinical interpretation included.

  • Glasgow, Braden, Morse, APGAR
  • Dose, drip rate and solution calculations
  • BMI, body surface area and more
Medication Database

More than 600 medications with all the information a professional needs at the point of care, without an internet connection.

  • Doses, routes of administration and frequency
  • Indications, contraindications and adverse effects
  • Specific nursing care considerations
Quick-Reference Guides & Protocols

More than 100 quick-reference guides and protocols organized for immediate access when you need them most.

  • Step-by-step nursing procedures
  • Urgency and emergency protocols
  • Updated clinical references
Care Plan Generator

Generate basic nursing care plans quickly and in a structured format directly from the app, ready to use.

  • Basic diagnoses and interventions
  • Clear and structured format
  • Complements Auxi Flow for complete NCP
